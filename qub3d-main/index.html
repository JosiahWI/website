WHO ARE WE?

You have reached the internet home of the Qub³d Engine Group!

We're currently building all sorts of awesome things, including the Qub³d Engine, a brand new voxel game engine for C++ and OpenGL & Vulkan. We'll also be creating an epic default game for Sandblox, currently entitled Horizons.

WHERE ARE WE?

Our development platform is PHABRICATOR. (We also have GitHub Mirrors) We use Discord as our main communication platform, and Mattermost as a secondary platform for those who can't use Discord.
